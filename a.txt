Dr. APJ abdul Kalam - The missile man, people's precident
Dr. APJ abdul Kalam’s  full name was Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam  
Dr. Kalam was responsible for the development of India’s first satellite launch vehicle, the SLV- 3  
Abdul Kalam was appointed as the chairman of Technology Information, Forecasting and Assessment council (TIFAC) 
He guided a number of Technology projects and missions to take India into the 21st century
