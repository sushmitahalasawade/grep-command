APJ Abdul Kalam was elected as the 11th president of India in 2002 
he held various positions under ISRO and DRDO 
he became Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government Of India 
Abdul Kalam has received honorary doctorates from 30 universities and the country’s three highest civilian honours – Padma Bhushan (1981),Padma Vibhushan (1990) and Bharat Ratna (1997)
